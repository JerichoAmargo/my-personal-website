Bachelor of Science in Information Technology Major in Business Analytics

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology majoring in Business Analytics is a specialized program offered under the College of Informatics and Computing Sciences at Batangas State University - The National Engineering University, Alangilan Campus. This program is designed to equip students with the technical skills and analytical capabilities needed to thrive in the fast-evolving field of IT and business analytics.

Specializations:
  • Business Analytics: Focuses on data mining, statistical analysis, data visualization, and business intelligence, preparing students to transform data into actionable insights.
  • Network Technology: Emphasizes the design, implementation, and management of computer networks, ensuring students are proficient in network infrastructure and security.
  • Service Management: Concentrates on IT service delivery and management, equipping students with skills in service support, operations, and process improvement.
Key Highlights:
  • Curriculum: The program combines core IT subjects with specialized courses in the chosen area of specialization. This integration ensures that graduates are well-versed in both the technical and business aspects of their field.
  • Hands-On Learning: Emphasis is placed on practical, hands-on learning experiences. Students engage in projects, internships, and real-world case studies that prepare them for the demands of the industry.
  • Modern Facilities: Students have access to state-of-the-art computer labs and software tools that are essential for learning and practicing their specialization.
  • Experienced Faculty: The program is delivered by a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who bring a wealth of industry knowledge and academic expertise to the classroom.
  • Career Opportunities: Graduates are well-prepared for various career paths, including roles such as data analyst, network administrator, IT consultant, and service manager. The skills acquired in this program are highly sought after in industries such as finance, healthcare, retail, and technology.
  • Industry Collaboration: The program maintains strong ties with industry partners, providing students with opportunities for networking, internships, and job placements.
Batangas State University - The National Engineering University, Alangilan Campus

Batangas State University (BatStateU) is a premier institution in the Philippines, known for its excellence in engineering, technology, and applied sciences. The Alangilan Campus, situated in Batangas City, is one of the key campuses of BatStateU, emphasizing engineering education and research.

Key Highlights:
  • Reputation: BatStateU - Alangilan Campus is renowned for its rigorous engineering programs and has earned the title of "The National Engineering University."
  • Facilities: Equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, research centers, and modern classrooms, the Alangilan Campus provides students with an excellent learning environment.
  • Faculty: The campus boasts a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members dedicated to providing top-notch education and mentorship to their students.
  • Research and Innovation: BatStateU encourages a strong culture of research and innovation, fostering collaborations with industries and other academic institutions to drive technological advancements and solutions.
  • Information Technology and Computer Science: The IT and Computer Science programs are designed to produce highly skilled professionals proficient in the latest technologies and methodologies, preparing students for successful careers in the tech industry.
